What is Dopamine?

Dopamine is your brain's “that felt good, let's do it again” messenger. It helps wire up motivation, learning, and focus, but it also keeps movement and mood on track. It isn't “good” or “bad” by itself. Tiny and balanced pulses help you chase goals and achieve dreams, while constant spikes can quickly turn into endless scrolling and quick fixes that crowd long term goals. The sweet spot is using dopamine as fuel for habits you actually care about, not letting it drag you around. It should be seen as a coach, not a boss.
